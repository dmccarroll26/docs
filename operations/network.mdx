Most cross-platform issues — payments failing, kiosks offline, dockets not printing — trace back to network problems. Work through this page before escalating any platform-specific issue.

<Note>
  All store devices (POS, Five.ID terminals, kiosks, KDS, printers) must be on the same store Wi-Fi network. Mixed networks are the cause of many "won't connect" issues.
</Note>

## Wi-Fi down or unstable

### iPad shows no Wi-Fi connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check other devices">
    Determine whether it's the iPad or the network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Wi-Fi on iPad">
    Settings → Wi-Fi → off, wait 10 seconds, on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Forget and reconnect">
    Tap (i) → Forget. Password is in the store iPad folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the router">
    If all devices are offline. See below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Wi-Fi connected but internet not working

<Steps>
  <Step title="Test in Safari">
    Load google.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the router">
    If the page won't load.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Lightspeed status">
    status.lightspeedhq.com on your phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to offline mode">
    If Lightspeed servers are down. Escalate immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Router restart procedure

<Warning>
  A router restart will temporarily disconnect **all** devices in the store (POS, Five terminals, kiosks, KDS, printers). Only restart at a natural pause or when the network is already fully down.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the router">
    Usually in the back office or above the ceiling tiles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unplug power">
    Pull the power cable from the router.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait 60 seconds">
    Use your phone timer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plug back in">
    Reconnect the power cable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for boot">
    3–5 minutes; lights should stabilise.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect devices in order">
    iPad → Five terminal → kiosks → printers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test transactions">
    On both till and kiosk.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Backup payment processing

If the store network is fully down and there's no mobile hotspot available, switch to **Square** backup terminals. See [Square](/platforms/square) for the full procedure.

## Need help?

If the network can't be restored, contact **Dave (Ops / Tech)**. For broadband outages or router replacement, Dave will direct you to your **Internet Provider** (details in the store setup sheet). See [Escalation contacts](/operations/escalation).
