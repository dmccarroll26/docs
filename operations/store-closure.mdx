If you need to close the store due to a POS or tech failure, follow this checklist.

<Warning>
  Don't close unilaterally. Notify your **Area Manager** first and wait for confirmation before closing trade.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Notify your Area Manager">
    Immediately, with details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify Dave (Ops / Tech)">
    Include details of the issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage in-store customers">
    Apologise and direct them to the nearest Sprout location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display closure signage">
    Use the Temporarily Closed sign from the back office.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Power off kiosks">
    Add Out of Order signs to each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pause delivery platforms">
    Deliveroo, Just Eat, Uber Eats — pause all tablets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay in store">
    Do not leave unattended — wait for confirmation from your Area Manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document everything">
    Time of closure, issue details, steps taken, customer impact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm before reopening">
    Notify your Area Manager before resuming trade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a test transaction">
    On both till and kiosk before serving the first customer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
