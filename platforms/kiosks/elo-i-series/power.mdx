Power-related issues with the Elo I-Series kiosk hardware.

<Tip>
  Power LED reference: **white** = on · **red** = off · **blinking** = sleep mode.
</Tip>

### Kiosk stuck on a white or black screen

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check power LED">
    White = on, red = off, blinking = sleep.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wake from sleep">
    Tap the screen or press power once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Force restart">
    Hold power for 10 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the cable">
    If no light, ensure power is connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    If unresolved, contact Dave / Kurve support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Kiosk not turning on

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the LED">
    Any light = power.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check cables">
    Kiosk and wall socket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different socket">
    If available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    If no LED, the power supply may have failed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign and redirect">
    Out of Order sign; direct customers to till.
  </Step>
</Steps>
