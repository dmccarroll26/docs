Display and touch issues with the Elo I-Series 22" Android touchscreen.

### Touch screen not responding accurately

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clean the screen">
    Soft, dry cloth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Elo">
    Cycle power.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recalibration may be needed">
    Escalate to Dave.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Don't recalibrate yourself">
    Could make the issue worse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Screen brightness very low or display flickering

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open admin panel">
    Hold home 3 seconds → passcode `925790`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check brightness">
    Adjust if available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart if flickering">
    Cycle the Elo unit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    May be a hardware fault.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need help?

If this hasn't resolved your issue, contact **Dave (Ops / Tech)**. See [Escalation contacts](/operations/escalation).
