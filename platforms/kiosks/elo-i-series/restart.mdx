Standard restart procedures for the Elo I-Series. Try a soft restart first; only use a hard restart if the soft restart fails.

### Soft restart

<Steps>
  <Step title="Press and hold the power button">
    Located on the rear/underside of the Elo unit. Hold for 3 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait 10 seconds">
    Let the screen go fully dark.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press power once">
    The Elo should boot and Kurve should auto-launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Hard restart

<Steps>
  <Step title="Unplug power">
    Disconnect the power cable from the back of the kiosk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait 30 seconds">
    Allow the unit to fully discharge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Replug">
    The Elo will boot; Kurve should auto-launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>
