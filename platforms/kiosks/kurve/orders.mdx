If kiosk orders aren't making it to the kitchen, the issue is almost always between the kiosk and the **Active iPad** — the bridge between Kurve and the docket printer.

### Customer completes order on kiosk but kitchen doesn't receive it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Active iPad first">
    See [Lightspeed → Active iPad](/platforms/lightspeed/active-ipad). It bridges kiosk and docket printer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Are orders on the Active iPad?">
    If yes but not printing, see [Docket printer](/platforms/instore-printers/docket-printer).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Are orders missing entirely?">
    Kiosk-to-POS issue. Check kiosk Wi-Fi.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Same network">
    Kiosk and Active iPad must share the store Wi-Fi.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Kurve back office">
    For queued or failed orders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the Elo">
    Test with a new order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Workaround">
    Direct customers to the main till; post Out of Order sign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    Dave / Kurve support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Orders arriving with missing items or wrong details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Don't fix at store level">
    Kurve menu-sync issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take a photo">
    Capture KDS/docket and kiosk screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send to Kurve Support">
    WhatsApp `+44 117 325 3256` and escalate to Dave.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take orders at till">
    Until resolved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need help?

If this hasn't resolved your issue, contact **Dave (Ops / Tech)**. See [Escalation contacts](/operations/escalation).
