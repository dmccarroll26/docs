The Elo I-Series 22" Android touchscreen is the hardware your Kurve kiosk runs on. This page covers hardware issues. For software problems (frozen Kurve app, payments, orders), see [Kurve](/platforms/kiosks/kurve).

## Power & boot issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Kiosk stuck on a white or black screen">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check power LED">
        White = on, red = off, blinking = sleep.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wake from sleep">
        Tap the screen or press power once.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Force restart">
        Hold power for 10 seconds.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check the cable">
        If no light, ensure power is connected.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        If unresolved, contact Dave / Kurve support.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Kiosk not turning on">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check the LED">
        Any light = power.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check cables">
        Kiosk and wall socket.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Try a different socket">
        If available.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        If no LED, the power supply may have failed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign and redirect">
        Out of Order sign; direct customers to till.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Display issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Touch screen not responding accurately">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Clean the screen">
        Soft, dry cloth.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart Elo">
        Cycle power.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Recalibration may be needed">
        Escalate to Dave.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Don't recalibrate yourself">
        Could make the issue worse.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Screen brightness very low or display flickering">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open admin panel">
        Hold home 3 seconds → passcode `925790`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check brightness">
        Adjust if available.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart if flickering">
        Cycle the Elo unit.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        May be a hardware fault.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Restart procedures

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Soft restart">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Press and hold the power button">
        Located on the rear/underside of the Elo unit. Hold for 3 seconds.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait 10 seconds">
        Let the screen go fully dark.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Press power once">
        The Elo should boot and Kurve should auto-launch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hard restart">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Unplug power">
        Disconnect the power cable from the back of the kiosk.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait 30 seconds">
        Allow the unit to fully discharge.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Replug">
        The Elo will boot; Kurve should auto-launch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Power LED reference: **white** = on · **red** = off · **blinking** = sleep mode.
</Tip>
