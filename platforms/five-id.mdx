All Sprout stores use Five.ID terminals for card payments and Leat loyalty scanning.

<Note>
  The Five terminal and the iPad **must** be on the same Wi-Fi network for payments to work.
</Note>

## Card reader connection & hardware

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="“Card reader not connected” error">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check the cable">
        Firmly connected at both ends.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reseat the cable">
        Disconnect and reconnect.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Power-cycle the terminal">
        Unplug, wait 15 seconds, replug.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Use the correct socket">
        Always the socket marked with a **Five sticker**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Card reader screen unresponsive or stuck">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Reset with a pin">
        Press the reset hole on the side of the reader with a SIM ejector or paperclip.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for boot">
        Let the device fully restart before retrying.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        If still stuck, contact Five.ID support.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Terminal not turning on or repeatedly restarting">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check the socket">
        Must be a Five-stickered socket.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Avoid loop-restarts">
        Other sockets can cause power instability.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Try another Five socket">
        If available.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        Contact Five.ID support.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## QR code / loyalty scanning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="QR code scanning not working (Leat loyalty)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Position the code">
        Hold the QR code 10–20 cm from the sensor.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Brighten the screen">
        Customer's phone screen brightness up, code clearly displayed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Clean the lens">
        Dry cloth.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Manual fallback">
        Enter the customer's loyalty details in Leat using their phone number.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Terminal network issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="iPad or terminal not connected to internet (red error)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check store Wi-Fi">
        See [Network & Wi-Fi](/operations/network).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Use an alternative network">
        Mobile hotspot if main Wi-Fi is down.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Same network for both">
        iPad and terminal **must** share the same Wi-Fi.
      </Step>
      <Step title="No network available">
        See [Square backup](/platforms/square).
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How to change Wi-Fi on the Five terminal">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open settings">
        Tap the Five home screen 3 times quickly.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter the password">
        `GiveMeFive!`
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open Wi-Fi settings">
        Tap **Open WiFi Settings**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect to network">
        Choose the alternative network.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Exit settings">
        Long-press the FLIC button, or unplug and replug.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Match the iPad">
        Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Transaction failures

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Card declined but customer says funds are available">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Try another card">
        Or contactless via phone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Retry">
        First attempts can time out.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test with a staff card">
        Confirm whether other transactions go through.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Network check">
        If all are failing, see [Network & Wi-Fi](/operations/network).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Never override">
        Do not manually process a declined transaction.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction timeout">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Likely network">
        Slow or unstable connection.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Retry">
        Customer taps/inserts again.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the terminal">
        Unplug, wait 15 seconds, replug — Five-stickered socket.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Wi-Fi signal">
        Restart the router if weak.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        Contact Five.ID support after 15 minutes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contactless not working (chip/PIN still works)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Clean the reader">
        Dry cloth.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Hold steady">
        Card flat for 2–3 seconds.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reset the reader">
        Pin hole on the side.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fall back to chip & PIN">
        Until the terminal is replaced.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log a Five.ID ticket">
        Request a replacement.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  **Refund note:** If a transaction fails mid-way and the customer is charged, do **not** attempt a manual refund at the till. Contact Five.ID support to confirm the transaction status before taking any action.
</Warning>

## Quick reference

| Action | How to |
| --- | --- |
| Reset card reader | Insert a pin into the reset hole on the side of the reader |
| Access Wi-Fi settings | Tap home screen 3 times quickly → Password: `GiveMeFive!` → Open WiFi Settings |
| Exit settings | Long-press the FLIC button, or unplug and replug the terminal |
| Correct power socket | Always use the socket marked with a Five sticker |
| QR code scanning distance | Hold QR code 10–20 cm from the sensor |
| Five.ID Support | support@five.id · +44 7916 468 891 |
