Sprout stores use **Epson TM-m30III** thermal printers for both receipts and kitchen dockets. Both connect via Bluetooth and have blue status lights.

<Note>
  The receipt printer pairs with the till iPad. The docket printer pairs with the **Active iPad** — if it stops printing dockets, check the [Active iPad](/platforms/lightspeed#the-active-ipad-local-server) first.
</Note>

## Receipt printer (under till)

The receipt printer sits under the till counter and connects via Bluetooth to the till iPad.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not printing / no output">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check power">
        Solid blue power light.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Bluetooth light">
        Solid blue. If off, the printer is disconnected.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check paper">
        Replace the roll if empty.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Lightspeed">
        Settings → Printers.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the printer">
        Power off, wait 15 seconds, power on; let it re-pair.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check iPad Bluetooth">
        Settings → Bluetooth → confirm Epson is connected.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Re-pair if needed">
        Forget the device and re-pair.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test print">
        From Lightspeed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Printing blank receipts">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check roll orientation">
        Thermal (smooth) side faces outward.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reload">
        Flip and reload.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test print">
        Confirm.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Faded or partial printing">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Replace the roll">
        Likely running low.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Clean the print head">
        Lint-free cloth, no liquids.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate">
        Print head may need replacement.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bluetooth keeps disconnecting">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check range">
        5–10 metres, obstacles can interfere.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart both devices">
        Printer and iPad.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Forget and re-pair">
        Settings → Bluetooth → (i) → Forget This Device.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Avoid pairing conflicts">
        The Epson pairs with one device at a time.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate to Dave">
        If drops continue.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Digital line docket printer

The docket printer is paired via Bluetooth to the **Active iPad**. For dockets to print, the Active iPad must have Lightspeed open and the Active Device must be online.

<Warning>
  If dockets stop printing, the Active iPad is the **first** thing to check. See [Lightspeed → Active iPad](/platforms/lightspeed#the-active-ipad-local-server).
</Warning>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Kitchen not receiving dockets">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check power">
        Solid blue power light.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Bluetooth light">
        If off, the printer has lost its connection to the iPad.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Lightspeed must be open">
        On the paired iPad.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Active Device must be online">
        Critical: Settings → Active Device must show online.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the printer">
        Power off, wait 15 seconds, power on; let it re-pair.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check iPad Bluetooth">
        Settings → Bluetooth.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Re-pair if needed">
        Forget the device and re-pair.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test docket">
        From Lightspeed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lightspeed Active Device is offline">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Active Device settings">
        Settings → Active Device on the Active iPad.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Bring it back online">
        Tap the toggle.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reopen Lightspeed">
        If it won't come online.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the iPad">
        If still offline.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm with a test order">
        Verify the docket prints.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Printing blank dockets">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check roll orientation">
        Thermal side outward.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reload">
        Flip and reload.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Test docket">
        Confirm.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bluetooth keeps disconnecting from docket printer">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check range">
        5–10 metres.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart both devices">
        Printer and paired iPad.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Forget and re-pair">
        Settings → Bluetooth → (i) → Forget This Device.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Single-device pairing">
        The Epson pairs with one device at a time.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Escalate to Dave">
        If drops continue.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Wrong items printing on dockets">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Configuration issue">
        Printer routing in Lightspeed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check routing">
        Configuration → Printers → Routing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Don't change it yourself">
        Escalate to Dave for a central fix.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
