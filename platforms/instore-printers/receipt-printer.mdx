The Epson TM-m30III receipt printer sits under the till counter and pairs via Bluetooth to the till iPad. Status lights are blue.

### Not printing / no output

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check power">
    Solid blue power light.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Bluetooth light">
    Solid blue. If off, the printer is disconnected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check paper">
    Replace the roll if empty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Lightspeed">
    Settings → Printers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the printer">
    Power off, wait 15 seconds, power on; let it re-pair.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check iPad Bluetooth">
    Settings → Bluetooth → confirm Epson is connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-pair if needed">
    Forget the device and re-pair.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test print">
    From Lightspeed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Printing blank receipts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check roll orientation">
    Thermal (smooth) side faces outward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload">
    Flip and reload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test print">
    Confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Faded or partial printing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Replace the roll">
    Likely running low.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clean the print head">
    Lint-free cloth, no liquids.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    Print head may need replacement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bluetooth keeps disconnecting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check range">
    5–10 metres, obstacles can interfere.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart both devices">
    Printer and iPad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Forget and re-pair">
    Settings → Bluetooth → (i) → Forget This Device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Avoid pairing conflicts">
    The Epson pairs with one device at a time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate to Dave">
    If drops continue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
