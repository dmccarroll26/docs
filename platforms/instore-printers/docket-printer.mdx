The digital line docket printer is an Epson TM-m30III paired via Bluetooth to the **Active iPad**. For dockets to print, the Active iPad must have Lightspeed open and the Active Device must be online.

<Warning>
  If dockets stop printing, the Active iPad is the **first** thing to check. See [Lightspeed → Active iPad](/platforms/lightspeed/active-ipad).
</Warning>

### Kitchen not receiving dockets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check power">
    Solid blue power light.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Bluetooth light">
    If off, the printer has lost its connection to the iPad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lightspeed must be open">
    On the paired iPad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Active Device must be online">
    Critical: Settings → Active Device must show online.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the printer">
    Power off, wait 15 seconds, power on; let it re-pair.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check iPad Bluetooth">
    Settings → Bluetooth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-pair if needed">
    Forget the device and re-pair.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test docket">
    From Lightspeed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Lightspeed Active Device is offline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Active Device settings">
    Settings → Active Device on the Active iPad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bring it back online">
    Tap the toggle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reopen Lightspeed">
    If it won't come online.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the iPad">
    If still offline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with a test order">
    Verify the docket prints.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Printing blank dockets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check roll orientation">
    Thermal side outward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload">
    Flip and reload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test docket">
    Confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bluetooth keeps disconnecting from docket printer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check range">
    5–10 metres.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart both devices">
    Printer and paired iPad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Forget and re-pair">
    Settings → Bluetooth → (i) → Forget This Device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Single-device pairing">
    The Epson pairs with one device at a time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate to Dave">
    If drops continue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Wrong items printing on dockets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configuration issue">
    Printer routing in Lightspeed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check routing">
    Configuration → Printers → Routing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Don't change it yourself">
    Escalate to Dave for a central fix.
  </Step>
</Steps>
