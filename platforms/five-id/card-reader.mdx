Hardware-level issues with the Five.ID card reader — connection errors, stuck screens, and power problems.

### "Card reader not connected" error

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the cable">
    Firmly connected at both ends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reseat the cable">
    Disconnect and reconnect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Power-cycle the terminal">
    Unplug, wait 15 seconds, replug.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the correct socket">
    Always the socket marked with a **Five sticker**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Card reader screen unresponsive or stuck

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset with a pin">
    Press the reset hole on the side of the reader with a SIM ejector or paperclip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for boot">
    Let the device fully restart before retrying.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    If still stuck, contact Five.ID support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Terminal not turning on or repeatedly restarting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the socket">
    Must be a Five-stickered socket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Avoid loop-restarts">
    Other sockets can cause power instability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another Five socket">
    If available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    Contact Five.ID support.
  </Step>
</Steps>
