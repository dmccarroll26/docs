The Five terminal and the iPad must be on the **same** Wi-Fi network for payments to work. Most network errors come down to that.

### iPad or terminal not connected to internet (red error)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check store Wi-Fi">
    See [Network & Wi-Fi](/operations/network).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use an alternative network">
    Mobile hotspot if main Wi-Fi is down.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Same network for both">
    iPad and terminal **must** share the same Wi-Fi.
  </Step>
  <Step title="No network available">
    See [Square backup](/platforms/square).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to change Wi-Fi on the Five terminal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Tap the Five home screen 3 times quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the password">
    `GiveMeFive!`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Wi-Fi settings">
    Tap **Open WiFi Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to network">
    Choose the alternative network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exit settings">
    Long-press the FLIC button, or unplug and replug.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match the iPad">
    Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi.
  </Step>
</Steps>
