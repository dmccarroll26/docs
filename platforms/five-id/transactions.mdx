Failures during card transactions — declines, timeouts, and contactless issues.

<Warning>
  **Refund note:** If a transaction fails mid-way and the customer is charged, do **not** attempt a manual refund at the till. Contact Five.ID support to confirm the transaction status before taking any action.
</Warning>

### Card declined but customer says funds are available

<Steps>
  <Step title="Try another card">
    Or contactless via phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry">
    First attempts can time out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test with a staff card">
    Confirm whether other transactions go through.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Network check">
    If all are failing, see [Network & Wi-Fi](/operations/network).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Never override">
    Do not manually process a declined transaction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Transaction timeout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Likely network">
    Slow or unstable connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry">
    Customer taps/inserts again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart the terminal">
    Unplug, wait 15 seconds, replug — Five-stickered socket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Wi-Fi signal">
    Restart the router if weak.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate">
    Contact Five.ID support after 15 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Contactless not working (chip/PIN still works)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clean the reader">
    Dry cloth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hold steady">
    Card flat for 2–3 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset the reader">
    Pin hole on the side.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fall back to chip & PIN">
    Until the terminal is replaced.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log a Five.ID ticket">
    Request a replacement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
